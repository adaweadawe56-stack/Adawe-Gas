Privacy Policy

Your trust matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Adawe Gas Network collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information while providing a safe and reliable LPG marketplace across Kenya.

Last Updated • July 17, 2026

Privacy Policy for Adawe Gas Network

Welcome to Adawe Gas Network. Your privacy is extremely important to us. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring transparency about how your data is collected, used, stored, and shared whenever you use our website or services.

Our Commitment: We never sell your personal information to third parties. Your information is only used to provide secure LPG marketplace services and improve your experience.

📋 1. Information We Collect

When you use Adawe Gas Network, we may collect the following information to provide our services efficiently and securely.

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Delivery Address
  • GPS Location (only if you grant permission)
  • LPG Brand and Cylinder Size
  • Order Details
  • Assigned Seller Information
  • Ratings and Reviews
  • Device, Browser and Technical Information

If you register as an LPG Seller, we may also collect:

  • Business Name
  • Email Address
  • Business Phone Number
  • Business Location
  • LPG Prices
  • Account Information

⚙️ 2. How We Use Your Information

Adawe Gas uses your information only for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Processing LPG delivery orders.
  • Connecting customers with nearby LPG sellers.
  • Displaying real-time order tracking.
  • Providing customer support.
  • Improving website performance.
  • Preventing fraud and abuse.
  • Maintaining marketplace security.
  • Improving overall user experience.

📍 3. Location Information

If you allow location access, Adawe Gas may use your GPS location to:

  • Find nearby LPG sellers.
  • Calculate delivery distance.
  • Estimate delivery time (ETA).
  • Improve delivery accuracy.
Location access is optional. You may continue using Adawe Gas without enabling location services, although some features may become less accurate.

🔒 4. Sharing Your Information

Adawe Gas only shares information necessary to complete your LPG order.

Your information may be shared with:

  • The seller assigned to your order.
  • Trusted service providers that help operate our platform.
  • Government authorities where required by Kenyan law.
Important: Adawe Gas never sells your personal information to advertisers or unrelated third parties.

🛡️ 5. Data Security

Protecting your information is a priority.

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against:

  • Unauthorized access.
  • Data loss.
  • Misuse.
  • Unauthorized disclosure.
  • Cybersecurity threats.

Although we work hard to protect your information, no online platform can guarantee absolute security.

⭐ 6. Ratings & Reviews

Customers may leave ratings and reviews after successfully completed orders.

Reviews may include:

  • Star Rating
  • Review Comment
  • Seller Name

Personal phone numbers, email addresses and sensitive customer information are never displayed publicly.

🏪 7. Seller Responsibilities

Adawe Gas is an independent digital marketplace.

Every registered LPG seller remains fully responsible for:

  • LPG quality.
  • Pricing.
  • Delivery.
  • Customer service.
  • Compliance with Kenyan LPG regulations.
  • Business licenses and legal requirements.
Adawe Gas connects buyers and sellers but does not manufacture, refill or distribute LPG cylinders.

🍪 8. Cookies

Adawe Gas may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve website performance.
  • Remember your preferences.
  • Provide a better browsing experience.
  • Maintain website security.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 9. Children's Privacy

Adawe Gas services are intended for individuals aged 18 years or older.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 years of age.

📝 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, legal obligations, or platform improvements.

The latest version will always be published on this page together with the updated revision date.
